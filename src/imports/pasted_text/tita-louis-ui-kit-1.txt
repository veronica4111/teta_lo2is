Design a complete UI/UX kit in Figma for "تيتا لوئيس" (Tita Louis), an Arabic
(Egyptian dialect) RTL educational OBSTACLE AVOIDANCE racing game for children
ages 4-10. This is NOT a lap-based race — it is an endless-runner / lane-dodging
survival game where a car drives down a straight road, dodging incoming obstacle
cars while fuel continuously drains, with a "Gas Station" quiz checkpoint at 25%
fuel. The game has 6 levels, each with unique story video, quiz content, and
audio.

All UI text must be in Arabic, written in EGYPTIAN ARABIC dialect (e.g., use
"كويس" instead of formal "جيد", "تعالى نلعب" instead of overly formal phrasing),
with RTL layout direction throughout (back buttons on the right, progress
indicators filling right-to-left, mirrored HUD placement).

Canvas sizes: Primary = mobile landscape 844x390px. Secondary variant = desktop/
tablet landscape 1280x720px. (Both variants are optimized for landscape orientation).

=====================================================================
SCREEN 1: RACE_RUNNING (Main Obstacle Avoidance Gameplay View)
=====================================================================
Match the composition of a third-person, behind-the-car view of a straight
road receding toward the horizon — exactly like a classic arcade obstacle-
dodging racer designed for a wide landscape aspect ratio:

- Background (top ~35%): sky gradient from light blue at top to near-white at
  the horizon, with a few soft cartoon clouds scattered across the wide view.
- Midground: rolling green hills along the horizon line, framed by decorative
  scenery — palm trees, leafy trees, small rock formations, and patches of
  greenery — lining both sides of the road. These scroll slowly for a parallax
  effect.
- Road: a straight grey asphalt road in 3-point perspective (narrow at the
  horizon, wide at the bottom), with a white dashed centerline and solid white
  edge lines on both sides. Design the road with 3 visible lanes for dodging,
  spaced comfortably across the wide screen.
- Obstacle cars: place 2-3 colored obstacle cars in different lanes at varying
  distances — smaller/higher near the horizon, larger/lower as they approach
  the player — representing the cars the player must avoid colliding with.
- Player car: large, bottom-center, only the top/back half visible (cropped by
  the bottom screen edge), in a bright kid-friendly color (red or orange),
  positioned in one of the 3 lanes with clear visual indication it can shift
  left/right.

HUD overlay (RTL-mirrored):
- Top-right: Fuel gauge — horizontal bar with fuel-pump icon, color-coded
  (green above 50%, yellow 25-50%, red below 25%).
- Top-left: Score counter in large Arabic-Indic or Arabic numerals.
- Top-center: small circular pause button.
- Optional: 3 small dot indicators showing the player's current lane.

Design 3 frames for this screen:
1. "04_السباق_عادي" — normal driving, fuel above 25%, neutral HUD colors.
2. "04_السباق_وقود_منخفض" — fuel between 5-25%, fuel gauge pulsing red, subtle
   red vignette at screen edges.
3. "04_السباق_وقود_حرج" — fuel below 5%, stronger red vignette, fuel gauge
   flashing, urgency cues.

=====================================================================
SCREEN 2: محطة الوقود (GAS_STATION — Quiz Checkpoint Modal)
=====================================================================
Triggers when fuel reaches 25% (one time per attempt). The race view freezes
(dimmed/slightly blurred background), and a landscape-optimized modal slides up 
from the bottom covering ~70-85% of the screen. The player answers 3 questions in sequence.

Modal header:
- Friendly cartoon gas-station illustration (fuel pump or small station
  building, matching the game's bright art style).
- Title in Egyptian Arabic: "محطة الوقود!" 
- Progress indicator showing "سؤال ١ من ٣" (Question 1 of 3), with 3 dot/
  segment markers in RTL order.

Question card (design for Q1, reusable for Q2/Q3):
- Large centered question text in Egyptian Arabic, RTL.
- Prominent audio-playback button with sound-wave icon (each question has its
  own narrated audio file — tap to hear the question read aloud).
- 2-4 large illustrated answer buttons with Arabic text, sized for small
  children's touch targets, arranged side-by-side or in a wide landscape layout.

Feedback states (shown briefly after each answer):
- CORRECT: green checkmark animation, "+٢٥٪ وقود" floating animation toward the
  fuel gauge, encouraging text "تمام!" or "برافو!"
- INCORRECT: gentle orange/neutral tone (not harsh red), no fuel change shown,
  encouraging text like "معلش، حاول تاني!" — then proceeds automatically to
  the next question (no retry of same question).

Footer/exit:
- After Question 3, fuel gauge animates filling based on total correct answers
  (0%, 25%, 50%, or 75% added, fuel capped at 100%).
- Short "هنرجع للسباق..." (Resuming race...) message with a 3-2-1 countdown
  before the modal slides away and gameplay resumes.

Design these frames:
- "05_محطة_الوقود_سؤال١_عادي"
- "05_محطة_الوقود_سؤال١_صحيح"
- "05_محطة_الوقود_سؤال١_غلط"
- (repeat naming pattern for سؤال٢ and سؤال٣)

=====================================================================
SCREEN 3: نتيجة السباق (RESULTS — Win/Loss)
=====================================================================
Two distinct landscape frames sharing the same button layout:

WIN ("06_النتيجة_فوز"):
- Bright celebratory background — confetti, stars, warm colors.
- Headline: "أحسنت يا بطل!" or "كسبت!"
- Large score display in Arabic numerals.
- Star rating (1-3 stars, filled based on score).
- Two buttons arranged horizontally: "إعادة المحاولة" (Retry) and "رجوع للمستويات" (Back to Levels).

LOSS ("06_النتيجة_خسارة"):
- Soft, encouraging (not punishing) illustration — car with empty fuel gauge,
  friendly character shrugging.
- Headline: "خلصت بنزين! جرب تاني" (You ran out of fuel! Try again) — warm,
  non-discouraging tone for young kids.
- Score reached still displayed, to motivate improvement.
- Same horizontal Retry / Back to Levels button layout as the WIN screen.

=====================================================================
SCREEN 4: اختيار المستوى (LEVEL_SELECT — 6 Levels)
=====================================================================
A horizontal carousel or a wide 3x2 grid layout showing 6 level tiles, each with:
- Unique placeholder thumbnail illustration labeled "رسم المستوى ١" through
  "رسم المستوى ٦".
- Level name in Arabic (e.g., "المستوى الأول", "المستوى الثاني", etc.).
- Star rating earned (0-3 stars, empty outline if unplayed).
- Optional lock icon overlay for locked levels (flag as a design decision).

Annotate clearly that each level tile, when tapped, loads a DIFFERENT story
video and a DIFFERENT set of 3 quiz questions (each with its own audio file) —
no content is shared between levels unless explicitly configured.

=====================================================================
SHARED COMPONENT LIBRARY PAGE
=====================================================================
Create a components page including:
- Fuel gauge component with 5 fill-state variants (0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
  and 3 color states (green/yellow/red).
- HUD buttons (pause, audio toggle).
- Answer option button (default / correct / incorrect states).
- Star rating component (0-3 stars).
- Primary/secondary CTA buttons (إعادة المحاولة, رجوع, استمرار).
- Color palette matching the reference: sky blue, grass green, asphalt grey,
  warm red/orange accents for player car and primary CTAs.
- Typography: a rounded, highly legible Arabic font suitable for children
  (e.g., Cairo, Tajawal, or Baloo Bhaijaan 2), paired with a friendly Latin/
  numeral font for scores.
